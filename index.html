Operation blink

Operation blink a tactics adventure game that I worked on for 4 months. I wanted to create a cross over with fire emblem and pokemon. It was originally a school project but I ended up coding 80% of it and doing all the art work. the level design was done by another student. overall with a two man team we end up with a pretty good prototype
